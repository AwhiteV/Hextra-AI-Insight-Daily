### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to keep tabs on activity on our Service and store certain info. The tracking technologies we use include beacons, tags, and scripts. They help us collect and track information, and also improve and analyze our Service. The technologies we use might include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a tiny file dropped on Your Device. You can tell Your browser to refuse all Cookies or to give You a heads-up when one's being sent. But hey, if You don't accept Cookies, some parts of our Service might not work for You. Our Service might use Cookies unless You've tweaked Your browser settings to reject them.
- **Web Beacons**: Some parts of our Service and our emails might contain tiny electronic files called web beacons (you might also hear them called clear gifs, pixel tags, or single-pixel gifs). These files let the Company, for instance, count users who've visited those pages or opened an email. They're also used for other website stats, like checking a section's popularity and verifying system and server integrity.

Cookies can be "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent ones stick around on Your personal computer or mobile device even after You go offline. Session Cookies, on the other hand, vanish the moment You close Your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the following purposes:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are super important for giving You the services available through the Website and letting You use some of its cool features. They help us authenticate users and stop any fishy business with user accounts. Without these Cookies, we simply can't provide the services You've asked for, and we only use them for that!

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies check if users have given the green light to use cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies let us remember the choices You make when You use the Website, like Your login info or language preference. The goal is to give You a more personalized experience and save You from typing in Your preferences every single time you visit!

For more info on the Cookies we use and Your choices about them, check out our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
